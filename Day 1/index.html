How to make a peanut butter and jam sandwich

  1. Get 2 bits of bread out
  2. Get the jam
  3. Spread jam on one piece of bread
  4. Get the peanut butter
  5. Spread peanut butter on the other piece of bread
  6. Put bread together (condiment sides inward)
  7. Eat the sandwich!
